His other prominent works include [Fire Force]({{ site.url_prefixes.myanimelist.anime }}/38671), [The Eminence in Shadow]({{ site.url_prefixes.myanimelist.anime }}/48316) and [Golden Kamuy]({{ site.url_prefixes.myanimelist.anime }}/36028).
