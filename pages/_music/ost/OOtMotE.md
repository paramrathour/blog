I can describe Ushio's works in one word --- [MADNESS]({{ site.url_prefixes.youtube.video }}/w_I1MWCTkyQ). His experimental way of incorporating non-standard musical instruments, such as 
[ping-pong balls]({{ site.url_prefixes.youtube.video }}/4z0331DziwA), 
pianos prepared using [erasers/nails]({{ site.url_prefixes.youtube.video }}/H2FukLl451w) or [footpedals]({{ site.url_prefixes.youtube.video }}/a57-KveTE84), and just weird [electronic]({{ site.url_prefixes.youtube.video }}/8J6MwUpSU3M?t=46) [noises]({{ site.url_prefixes.youtube.video }}/H3zMCVNRko4?t=43) in the composition sets him apart from his contemporaries. While [Ping Pong the Animation]({{ site.url_prefixes.myanimelist.anime }}/22135) and [Devilman: Crybaby]({{ site.url_prefixes.myanimelist.anime }}/35120) showcase some of his wildest works, his [ambi]({{ site.url_prefixes.youtube.video }}/_U4FQmeYXwc)[ent]({{ site.url_prefixes.youtube.video }}/SZJvCUrVesM) scores has also played an integral part in the success of [Chainsaw Man]({{ site.url_prefixes.myanimelist.anime }}/44511), [Dandadan]({{ site.url_prefixes.myanimelist.anime }}/57334), [Heavenly Delusion]({{ site.url_prefixes.myanimelist.anime }}/53393), _Orb: On the Movements of the Earth_, [The Dangers in My Heart]({{ site.url_prefixes.myanimelist.anime }}/52578) and films like [A Silent Voice]({{ site.url_prefixes.myanimelist.anime }}/28851), [Liz and the Blue Bird]({{ site.url_prefixes.myanimelist.anime }}/35677), [The Colors Within]({{ site.url_prefixes.myanimelist.anime }}/53747) and more.
