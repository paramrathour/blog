## Introduction
Grading is undoubtedly one of the most frustrating experience in a life of a college student. And I would assume even many professors also don't like the idea _drawing the line_ between their hard-working students. But as much as both parties do not like the process, it is a necessary step which helps in the evaluation. So how can it be improved?

Firstly, there are two popular grading systems: percentage-based and grade-point-based (CGPA). The policy I will be outlining assumes latter system but it can be easily tweaked to adapt to the former too. So, let's not worry about grading systems and deal with grades course-wise.
